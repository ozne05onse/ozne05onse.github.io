OUR PRODUCT

      Quail eggs are a hoarder of many nutrients and are, therefore, a safe option to include in your diet. Quail eggs are the midsized eggs laid by the various species of quail.They are a good source of many nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, etc. and provides a number of benefits.That's why we chose the quail egg as our main ingredient.

Quail Egg Can Improve Your Eyesight

      Eating quail eggs on a regular and moderate basis has been found to be very beneficial for improving our eyesight and for protecting our eyes from damage.This benefit of quail eggs is mainly because of the presence of antioxidants like “Vitamin A” in them. Vitamin A is a vital nutrient for our eyes and plays an important role in keeping them healthy.
